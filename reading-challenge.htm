An Experimental Supplimentary Reading Program

"The Reading Challenge" -- our 2016 Field Trial

As every experienced teacher knows, student attitude develops into student aptitude. The love of books is the soul of all education -- a firm foundation leading to a lifetime of learning. Although we have some amazing technology for optimizing the reading experience, our primary task is to help each student develop permanent enthusiasm towards challenging reading.

To achieve this end, we have combined the best tradition of aural storytelling with the most engaging stories. These are sequenced scientifically with vast data analysis and delivered with cutting edge interactive mobile app technologies. The result is a smart reading platform that delivers an adaptive curriculum optimizing each student's experience to ensure high comprehension, enjoyment and learning.

We have been developing our software for several years and it is now ready for some serious field testing. So we are launching a handful of pilot tests in diverse schools around the globe. Our initial program, "The Reading Challenge", is a light selection of about 50 books -- some of the most fun and engaging stories ever told. The program does not replace existing curriculum or classroom instruction but simply challenges participants to spend at least one hour each day reading these great stories. We will provide each student with a tablet computer pre-loaded with our software and we will track the progress and work of each student.

This field trial will begin in September 2016. If you have students who would like to participate in a six-month guided reading program, contact us. Simply call Peter at (617) 959-7591 or email at peter@firm-foundation.org

Our "FFA Reader" Guided-Reading Software

In a nutshell, our software, the FFA Reader, is a guided-reading tool aimed at accelerating the development of reading skills. It does this through providing an extensive and immersive reading experience -- while carefully tracking, analysing and adapting to each student's needs. Students are “guided” to learn new vocabulary and to take on books that will challenge them but are not beyond their capacity. “Immersive reading” is normal reading of text accompanied by audio that is aligned word-for-word with the text, providing a read-along experience which significantly boosts comprehension, enjoyment and retention.

The software periodically re-assess each student’s reading strength and reports progress directly to the teacher as well as to our data centers -- allowing us constantly fine-tune our effectiveness. Although we will carefully track each student's development of reading skills, yet our primary metric for success is how effectively we are able to create a habit of extra reading. After all, the habit of reading constantly is the greatest educational gift we can give to any student -- a solid foundation for an educated life.
