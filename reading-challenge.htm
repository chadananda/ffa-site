An Experimental Supplimentary Reading Program

Leveraging Technology to Boost Reading

FFA Reader is a guided reading platform aimed at accelerating the development of reading skills. It does this through providing an immersive reading experience while carefully tracking, analysing and adapting to each student's reading strength.

2016 Pilot, "Reading Challenge"

The FFA Reader core functionality is complete and it is time for some field trials. Over the next six months, we will be running a set of pilot tests in which students will be assigned a tablet computer and will engage in an hour or two of immersive reading each day. The software will regularly re-assess their reading strength and progress statistics will be reported back to their teacher and to our data centers, allowing us to fine-tune our program delivery.

If you have students who would like to participate in a six-month accelerated reading trial, contact us for more details.
